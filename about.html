About CISA KEV Deadline Planner

CISA KEV Deadline Planner is a free browser worksheet for matching pasted CVEs against a bundled CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog snapshot and exporting a remediation planning queue.

Boundaries

The tool runs entirely in the browser with a bundled local catalog file. It has no signup, cookies, analytics, beacons, uploads, browser storage, or external scripts.

This is an unofficial informational worksheet. It is not CISA affiliation, legal advice, security advice, remediation advice, a scanner, vulnerability validation, or environment-specific risk analysis.
